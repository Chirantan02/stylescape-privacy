Introduction

Welcome to Stylescape ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, profile photo

1.3 AI-Generated Content

  • Virtual Try-On Images: AI-generated outfit visualizations created from your photos
  • Processing Metadata: Technical data about image generation

Important: Virtual Try-On (VTON) images are processed temporarily for generation purposes only. We do not store, access, view, or sell VTON images.

Third-Party AI Processing: Virtual try-on images are generated using Replicate's AI infrastructure. Your photos are transmitted securely to Replicate for processing and deleted within 1 hour.

  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3.1 User Content

When you delete your account all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
